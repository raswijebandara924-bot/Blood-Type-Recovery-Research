# Blood-Type-Recovery-Research

## Research Title
The Influence of Blood Types on Perceived Recovery from Infectious Diseases Among University Students in Anuradhapura District, Sri Lanka

## Overview
This repository contains my undergraduate genetics research project investigating the relationship between ABO blood groups and perceived recovery from infectious diseases among university students in Sri Lanka.

## Research Design
- Cross-sectional study
- Sample size: 374 university students
- Data analysis using SPSS

- Research methodology
- Statistical analysis
